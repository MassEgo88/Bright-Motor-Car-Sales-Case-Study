# Bright-Motor-Car-Sales-Case-Study
This is my bright motor car sales analysis


## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing car sales data for **Bright Motors**, with the goal of uncovering key business insights that can improve sales performance, optimize inventory, and support strategic decision-making.

The analysis was conducted as part of a case study, where I worked as a **Data Analyst** to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through data cleaning, SQL analysis, and dashboard visualization.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze Bright Motors’ car sales data to:

* Identify top-performing vehicles
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Evaluate regional sales performance
* Analyze the impact of pricing and mileage
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve profitability

---

## Business Overview

Bright Motors is a multi-region vehicle dealership offering **budget, mid-range, and luxury vehicles**.

Operating in a competitive automotive market, the company aims to:

* Expand its dealership network
* Improve overall sales performance
* Optimize inventory using data-driven strategies

---

## Project Approach

### 1. Data Collection

* Source: Car Sales Dataset (CSV/Excel)
* Includes:

  * Make, Model, Year
  * Selling Price
  * Mileage
  * Region
  * Sales Date

---

### 2. Data Cleaning & Preparation

* Removed duplicates
* Handled missing values
* Converted price fields into numeric format
* Standardized column formats

### Calculated Metrics:

* **Total Revenue = Selling Price × Units Sold**
* **Profit Margin = (Selling Price − Cost Price) ÷ Selling Price**

---

### 3. Data Analysis

Key areas of analysis included:

* Revenue by car make and model
* Sales trends over time
* Regional performance
* Price vs mileage relationship
* Market segmentation (Budget vs Mid vs Luxury)

---

### 4. Data Visualization

An interactive dashboard was created to present insights clearly and effectively.

## Key Insights

* Sales fluctuate over time, indicating **seasonal demand patterns**
* **Budget vehicles dominate** the market, making up the largest share of sales
* Sales performance varies significantly across regions
* **Mileage has a strong impact on pricing and demand**
* Revenue is high overall, but not consistent across all periods

---

## Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies are recommended:

* Expand operations in **high-performing regions**
* Increase inventory of **budget vehicles** to match demand
* Introduce **promotions during low sales periods**
* Prioritize **low-mileage vehicles** for better pricing
* Implement **data-driven pricing strategies**
